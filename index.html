Proactive Hubble

Pattern detection and suggested actions before you even ask

Pattern & Signal Engine

Cross-system anomaly and opportunity detection that continuously analyzes signals.

What It Analyzes

  • Usage trend changes (drop-off, stalled onboarding)
  • Risk combinations (engagement + renewal + tickets)
  • Repeated friction signals across accounts
  • Expansion indicators (seat growth, feature limits)

Outputs

  • Signals with explanation & confidence level
  • Account-level flags (risk, plateau, expansion)
  • Segment-level patterns (systemic issues)

Recommended Actions

Playbooks generated from signals with the next best action for CSMs and Support.

What Hubble Suggests

  • Next best action (NBA) with rationale
  • Artifacts needed (talk track, checklist, links)
  • "Why this action now" explanation

Example Plays

  • Onboarding stalled → admin setup review
  • Feature unused → training + milestones
  • Ticket spike → troubleshooting + audit

Delivery & Learning Loop

Proactive surfaces and continuous improvement based on outcomes.

Delivery Surfaces

  • Proactive dashboard: "Today's accounts"
  • Nudges: "Account X trending down 14 days"
  • Workflow integrations (where teams work)

Learning Loop

  • Users rate usefulness of suggestions
  • Outcomes tracked (usage, tickets, renewal)
  • Learns which plays work by segment
